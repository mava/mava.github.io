Prerequisites

AMAT 113 or 119. (See the University Bulletin.)

Description

“Curves and vectors in the plane, geometry of three-dimensional space, vector functions in three-space, partial derivatives, multiple integrals, line and surface integrals.” (See the University Bulletin.)

Textbook

James Stewart, Multivariable Calculus, Seventh Edition, Brooks/Cole, Cengage Learning.

  • Chapter 12: Vectors and the Geometry of Space.
  • Chapter 13: Vector Functions.
  • Chapter 14: Partial Derivatives.
  • Chapter 15: Multiple Integrals.
  • Chapter 16: Vector Calculus.
