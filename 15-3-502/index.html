Prerequisites

Familiarity with undergraduate mathematics and some ability with computer code. (See the University Bulletin.)

Description

The goal of this course is to learn how to let computers help you do and teach math. In particular, you will learn how to use:

  • the typesetting system LaTeX, and
  • the mathematics software system Sage.

Textbook

None, but reading material will be distributed in class.

Grading

Weekly project assignments.

Students are expected to attend all class meetings. The maximum number of absences permitted to receive credit for this course is 5 (five). Excessive tardiness may count as absence.

Of course, students are expected to follow the University’s Standards of Academic Integrity.
